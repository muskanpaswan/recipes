masala Paratha

Today I made very tasty masala paratha Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2½ cup wheat flour / atta
  • ¾ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ¼ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp aamchur / dry mango powder
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi, crushed
  • ¼ tsp ajwain / carom seeds
  • ½ tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp ghee / clarified butter
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup hot water
  • 3 tbsp ghee / clarified butter
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ¼ cup wheat flour / atta, for dusting
  • oil for roasting

masala paratha

  1. firstly, in a large bowl take 2½ cup wheat flour and add ¾ tsp chilli powder, ½ tsp garam masala, ¼ tsp turmeric, ¼ tsp cumin powder.
  2. also add ½ tsp aamchur, 1 tbsp kasuri methi, ¼ tsp ajwain, ½ tsp ginger paste, 1 tsp ghee and 1 tsp salt.
  3. crumble and mix well making sure the spices are well combined.
  4. add 1 cup hot water and mix with the spoon.
  5. knead the dough well-adding water as required.
  6. grease the dough with oil and rest for 15 minutes.
  7. knead the dough again slightly and pinch a ball sized dough.
  8. dust with wheat flour and roll to small disc.
  9. spread 1 tsp ghee and pinch of garam masala.
  10. with the back of a spoon, spread uniformly.
  11. form a bundle forming a ball sized dough.
  12. also, dust with some wheat flour.
  13. further, roll it in a slightly thick circle like chapati or paratha.
  14. now on a hot tawa place the rolled paratha and cook for a minute.
  15. furthermore, when the base is partly cooked, flip the masala paratha.
  16. also, spread ½ tsp oil / ghee and press slightly both the sides.
  17. flip again once or twice till both the sides are cooked properly.
  18. finally, serve masala paratha with raita and pickle
