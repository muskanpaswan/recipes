vag manchurian

Today I made very tasty vag manchurian

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup shredded carrots
  • ¼ cup shredded cabbage
  • ¼ cup scallions thinly sliced
  • 2 tsp ginger grated
  • 2 tsp garlic grated
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 4 tbsp all purpose flour see notes
  • 1 tbsp things chili sauce
  • 1 tsp salt + pepper
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • ¼ cup onion finely chopped
  • 1 2 green chili finely minced
  • 2 tsp ginger grated
  • 2 tsp garlic grated
  • 1 tsp salt + pepper
  • 2 tbsp ketchups
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp chings chili sauce
  • 2 cups water
  • ½ tbsp cornstarch mixed with 3tbsp water for cornstarch slurry

Step vag manchurian

  1. In a large bowl, mix the cabbage, carrots, scallions ginger + garlic and salt + pepper.
  2. Next sprinkle in the corn starch and all purpose flour along with the chili sauce.
  3. Mix everything together with your hands until it starts to form into a dough like ball. *Add more flour if needed.
  4. Heat up a pot with 1inch deep oil.
  5. Roll out small balls and fry in small batches.
  6. Fry until the balls are golden brown and set them on a cooling rack.
  7. In a sauce pan, heat up the oil and add the onions and green chili.
  8. Cook for 1-2 mins on med high heat.
  9. Next add the ginger + garlic and salt + pepper. Cook for 30 seconds or until the ginger garlic is fragrant.
  10. Add the ketchup, soy sauce and chili sauce.
  11. Stir until everything is well incorporated and add in the water.
  12. Bring this to a simmer and add in the cornstarch slurry. Bring this to a low simmer and you’ll start to see the gravy gets thicken up a bit.
  13. Place the gravy in a bowl or deep plate and add the veg Manchurian balls, garnish with scallion and enjoy!
