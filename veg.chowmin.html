vag chowmin

Today I made very tasty vag chowmin

Ingredients

  • ½ cup maida / plain flour
  • ½ tsp salt
  • water for kneading
  • oil for greasing
  • 3 tsp oil
  • 3 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 chilli, finely chopped
  • 4 tbsp spring onion
  • 1 cup carrot, grated
  • 2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • ½ tsp pepper, crushed
  • ½ tsp salt

Step vag chowmin

  1. firstly, prepare stuffing by heating 3 tsp oil and saute 3 clove garlic, 1 inch ginger and 2 chilli.
  2. also, add 2 tbsp spring onion and saute on high flame.
  3. further, add 1 cup carrot and 2 cup cabbage. stir fry on high flame
  4. now add ½ tsp pepper and ½ tsp salt.
  5. additionally, 2 tbsp spring onion and stuffing mixture is ready.
  6. further, pinch a small ball sized momos dough and flatten.
  7. now dust with some maida and start to roll using a rolling pin.
  8. roll to almost medium thin circle. around 4
  9. 5 inch in diameter. make sure you roll from sides and keep the centre slightly thick
  10. now place a heaped tbsp of prepared stuffing in the centre.
  11. start pleating the edges slowly and gather everything.
  12. press in middle and seal the momos forming a bundle.
  13. heat a steamer and arrange the momos in the tray without touching each other.
  14. furthermore, steam momos for 10-12 minutes or till shiny sheen appears over it.
  15. finally, veg momos recipe is ready to enjoy with momos chutney.
